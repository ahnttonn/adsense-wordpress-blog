## How We Would Compare the Category
Compare ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Notion AI, Zapier, Otter, and Perplexity by the work they remove, the permissions they need, the quality controls they expose, and the cost of keeping them current. The strongest choice is rarely the longest feature list. A better launch-batch recommendation is to match one tool to one recurring workflow, then document where human review remains mandatory. For this category, prioritize source visibility, admin controls, export paths, and whether the assistant fits into existing work instead of creating another inbox.
