# Comparison Template

## Purpose
Use for ranked or side-by-side tool comparisons with criteria, verdicts, and alternatives.

## Required Editorial Blocks

1. Quick answer: two to four sentences that answer the search intent before any ad or long context.
2. Decision context: who this page is for, who should skip it, and what changed since the last review.
3. Evidence log: official product pages, help docs, pricing pages, changelogs, or benchmark notes used as data.
4. Main analysis: original synthesis in the publication voice; no copied competitor body structure.
5. Decision table or checklist: criteria, tradeoffs, and next actions the reader can scan.
6. Internal links: at least two relevant launch-batch slugs.
7. Review note: checked date, update cadence, and what must be rechecked before republishing.
